About RIBI

RIBI is an interediary between the Door, Donee and the Medical Center. We are focused on saving life by creating an avenue in which blood can be easily donated and the lives can be saved.

We provide the day to day communication between the individual who neds the blood and the donor who is ready to donate his/her blood to save a life. We are open to different Medical Centers who are ready to make this a successfully and assisting to save human lives by establishing a testing a center for the Donor and the Donee.
